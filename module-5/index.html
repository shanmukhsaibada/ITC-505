About Us

Welcome to CAFE DELIGHT, where we take pride in our commitment to organic coffee production. Located in the heart of FLAGSTAFF, we specialize in sourcing and extracting coffee powder using organic methods. Our beans are exclusively imported from the lush coffee plantations of Araku Valley in Andhra Pradesh, India. Renowned worldwide for its rich and aromatic coffee, Araku Valley guarantees the highest quality beans, ensuring each cup is a true delight for coffee enthusiasts. Join us on a journey of taste and sustainability, where every sip supports ethical farming practices and promotes the essence of Araku's renowned coffee culture. Discover the difference with CAFE DELIGHT today!.
